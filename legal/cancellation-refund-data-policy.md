# Cancellation, Refund & Data Policy

**DRAFT FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW.** Incorporated into the Subscription

## Cancellation

- Cancel anytime by written notice to team@triskope.ai from your account
  email. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid month;
  you keep full access until then.
- No long-term contract is required unless your Order Form states a term.

## Refunds

- Monthly fees are non-refundable once a billing month begins.
- Exception: if we materially fail to provide the Service for more than
  five (5) consecutive business days due to causes within our control,
  you may request a pro-rated credit for the affected period.
  [ATTORNEY: confirm remedy scope.]

## Suspension for non-payment

- Accounts more than 10 days past due (after notice) may be suspended:
  CRM access pauses, the website goes offline, and lead capture stops.
- Nothing is deleted during suspension. Full restoration upon payment.

## Your data at the end

- **During your subscription and for 30 days after termination**, you may
  export your leads and content from the Service or by request.
- **After the 30-day window**, we may permanently delete your workspace
  data. Backups purge on a rolling basis thereafter.
  [ATTORNEY: confirm retention numbers; align with any state obligations.]
- Your custom domain is yours; we will release DNS configuration
  cooperatively on request.
- We retain minimal records (invoices, consent/attestation logs) as
  required for legal and accounting purposes.

## Account deletion requests

Email team@triskope.ai from the account owner's address. Identity is
verified before destructive actions.
