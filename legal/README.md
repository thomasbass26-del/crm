# Triskope Legal Stack — Working Drafts

**Status: DRAFTS FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW. Not legal advice. Do not publish or
sign until reviewed by a South Carolina business attorney.**

Prepared for Triskope LLC (Myrtle Beach, SC), operator of The Market Edge
CRM (app.triskope.ai) and managed subscriber websites (<slug>.triskope.ai
and subscriber custom domains).

## Documents

| File | Purpose | Who signs/sees it |
|---|---|---|
| subscription-agreement.md | Master contract for the CRM + website service | Every subscriber, at signup |
| acceptable-use-policy.md | Rules for lead data, messaging, content | Incorporated into the agreement |
| early-access-addendum.md | Founding-subscriber terms | First 2–5 subscribers |
| cancellation-refund-data-policy.md | Cancellation, refunds, data export/deletion | Public policy page |

| site-terms-of-use-template.md | Per-subscriber website terms | Consumers on each subscriber site |

## Placeholders

Subscriber-site templates use {{TOKENS}} (agent name, brokerage, domain,
etc.) so they can be generated per tenant later — same pattern as
site_config.

number, and jurisdiction-sensitive clause is flagged inline.
