# DMCA / Copyright Policy

**DRAFT FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW.**

Triskope LLC respects intellectual property rights. Subscriber websites
hosted by Triskope display content provided by the subscriber; Triskope
responds to valid copyright complaints under the Digital Millennium
Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. § 512).

## Reporting infringement

Send a written notice to our designated agent including: (1) your
signature (physical or electronic); (2) identification of the
copyrighted work; (3) the URL of the allegedly infringing material;
(4) your contact information; (5) a good-faith statement that the use is
unauthorized; (6) a statement, under penalty of perjury, that the notice
is accurate and you are authorized to act.

**Designated agent:** Triskope LLC — DMCA Agent, team@triskope.ai
[ATTORNEY: register the designated agent with the U.S. Copyright Office
(online, ~$6) — required for safe-harbor protection.]

## Our process

Upon a valid notice we will remove or disable access to the material,
notify the subscriber, and accept counter-notices per § 512(g).
Repeat-infringer accounts may be terminated.
