# The Market Edge — Acceptable Use Policy

**DRAFT FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW.** Incorporated into the Subscription
Agreement. Violations may result in suspension or termination.

## 1. Lead Data & Consent

- Import or add only contacts you have a lawful basis to contact. When
  importing lists, you attest (and the Service records your attestation)
  that email/SMS consent was obtained where indicated.
- Do not import purchased lists lacking verifiable consent, scraped
  contact data, or data obtained in violation of another platform's
  terms.
- Honor opt-outs immediately. The Service enforces unsubscribe links on
  nurture email; you must not circumvent them.

## 2. Messaging (Email & SMS)

- Comply with CAN-SPAM: truthful headers/subjects, your valid identity,
  and working unsubscribe.
- SMS (when enabled) requires prior express written consent under the
  TCPA, recorded through the Service's consent capture. No SMS to
  numbers without recorded consent; honor STOP immediately; respect
  quiet hours required by law. [ATTORNEY: finalize with A2P registration
  language once carrier approval is obtained.]
- No deceptive, harassing, or unlawful messaging of any kind.

## 3. Website Content

- Only publish content you own or are licensed to use (photos, copy,
  logos, testimonials). Testimonials must be genuine and used with
  permission.
- Comply with Fair Housing laws in all content and advertising — no
  discriminatory statements or steering language.
- Display your brokerage affiliation as required by South Carolina
  Real Estate Commission rules and your brokerage's policies.
- Listing data must be accurate; MLS/IDX data (when available) must be
  displayed per the MLS's rules and not extracted or repurposed.

## 4. Platform Integrity

- No attempts to probe, bypass, or overload the Service's security or
  another workspace's data; no automated scraping of the platform; no
  reselling access; one workspace per brokerage team unless agreed.

## 5. Enforcement

We may remove content, pause features, or suspend accounts that violate
this policy, with notice where practicable. Violations creating legal
exposure (spam complaints, TCPA demands, copyright claims, Fair Housing
issues) may result in immediate suspension.
